Shuai Liao (廖帅)

Short Bio     [Full CV]

I am currently a 3rd year graduate student in Database & Intelligence Information Retrieval Lab (DB-IIR), School of Information, Renmin University of China (RUC), advised by Prof. Xiaoyong Du and Dr. Xirong Li.

I received a B.E. degree from the School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2012.

I was a research intern at Data & Knowledge Engineering Lab (DKE), School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering (ITEE), The University of Queensland, Australia (April-October, 2014), under supervision of Prof. Heng Tao Shen.

    Cross-Codebook Image classification
     Shuai Liao, Xirong Li, Xiaoyong Du
     The 2013 Pacific-Rim Conference on Multimedia (PCM).
     Nanjing, China. Dec. 13-16, 2013.
     Full Paper (Oral);    [pdf]  

       The popular bag of visual codes (BoVC) features image classification system depend on a precomputed codebook, when the codebook applied to test images differs from the codebook of an existing image classification system, the system becomes inapplicable. To resolve the codebook incompatibility problem, we propose cross-codebook image classification by BoVC feature transformation.
